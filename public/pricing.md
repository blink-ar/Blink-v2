# Pricing - Blink

Last updated: 2026-06-17

## Public Consumer App

- Price: Free to use
- Currency: ARS
- Billing period: None
- Account required: Not required for browsing public discounts; optional account features may exist for personalization
- Included: Search public bank discounts, card benefits, merchant pages, category pages, bank/category pages, map-based exploration, and saved preferences when signed in

## Paid Plans

- Self-serve paid plans: None listed
- Subscription tiers: None listed
- Enterprise or merchant pricing: Not publicly listed

## Notes

- Blink does not sell the bank benefits shown in the app.
- Discounts, caps, eligible cards, installment terms, and validity dates are controlled by the bank, wallet, or merchant offering each benefit.
- Users should confirm final terms with the issuing bank, wallet, or merchant before purchasing.
